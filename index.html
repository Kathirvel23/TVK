About Visil228

Digital Governance for Every Village

Visil228 is TVK Radhapuram's official citizen platform — a direct bridge between the 43 villages of this historic Tirunelveli constituency and their elected representative.

From the coastal hamlets of Chettikulam to the farming communities of Irukkandurai, every complaint is logged, tracked, and resolved with full accountability.

⚡

Faster Resolution

Submit directly to your ward representative. No middlemen, no waiting rooms, no ignored petitions.

🔍

Full Transparency

Real-time tracking on every request — from submission to closure. Every action is visible.

🏘️

Village-First Design

Built for Tamil Nadu's rural reality. Works on low-end phones, in Tamil, with minimal data usage.

Process

How Visil228 Works

Simple. Transparent. Accountable. From your phone to your MLA — in four steps.

01
📋

Submit Your Issue

Fill the form with your name, village, issue type, and description. Photo upload supported.

02
📍

Geo-Tagged & Logged

Your complaint is automatically tagged to your village and assigned a unique tracking ID.

03
🏃

Routed to Ward Rep

The nearest volunteer or area coordinator receives the complaint and takes field action.

04
✅

Resolved & Closed

You receive SMS/WhatsApp confirmation once your issue is resolved. All within 48 hours.

Submit Issue

Your Voice Matters

Every submission is logged, routed and tracked until it's solved. No passing the parcel — just service. Fill the form and we'll acknowledge within 48 hours.

  • Your issue is logged with a unique ID and timestamp
  • Nearest volunteer or coordinator is notified automatically
  • SMS/WhatsApp update at every stage of resolution
  • All data is kept private and used only for resolution
  • MLA's office reviews all unresolved issues weekly
